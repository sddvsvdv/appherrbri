Why recycling apps need local rules

Correct waste sorting is not only about material. The same object can have a different destination depending on its use, its packaging status and the local collection system.

  • Packaging may use a national packaging collection system.
  • Paper and cardboard can be collected separately or together with other recyclable packaging.
  • Glass bottles and jars are not always treated like drinking glasses or broken glass objects.
  • Medicines may require pharmacy return systems.
  • Electronics, batteries and lamps usually need special collection points.
  • Deposit-return containers may need a return machine or official return point.

Frequently asked questions

Basic information for international users who find EcoFot AI while searching for a recycling app.

Can I use EcoFot AI in any country?

No. EcoFot AI is limited to supported countries because recycling rules are local. It currently includes Spain, France, Portugal, Germany, Ireland, Chile, Austria and Poland. Use the country selector to choose the correct version.

Does the app decide only by material?

No. A plastic object, a plastic package and an electronic item with plastic parts may have different waste destinations depending on the local system.

Is EcoFot AI official?

EcoFot AI is an independent app by AplicacionesHerrbri. For hazardous waste, special waste or unclear cases, users should also check official local guidance.

Why is my country not supported yet?

Each country needs its own recycling configuration. Countries such as the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada or Australia may require separate local rules before being supported.
