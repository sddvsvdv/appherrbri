Not sure where that waste should go?

EcoFot AI is an Android app created to help with recycling. Take a photo of a waste item and get a simple suggestion using artificial intelligence and configured waste rules.

The app is not a chatbot. It is designed to give a direct answer: the recommended destination, a colour and a short explanation.

Why local rules matter

Recycling is not just about material. The same object can have a different destination depending on whether it is packaging, glass, textile, electronic waste, medicine, deposit-return waste or special waste.

  • Packaging may follow a specific national collection system.
  • Glass bottles are not always treated like broken drinking glasses.
  • Medicines may need pharmacy return systems.
  • Electronics usually require special collection points.
  • Deposit containers may need a return machine or return point.
