VolumTransport Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13/10/2024

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The VolumTransport app does not collect, store, or share any sensitive personal information (such as name, email, or payment details) from users. However, the app uses Google Analytics to collect anonymized data related to usage patterns and device information, which may include non-identifiable information such as device identifiers and approximate location for analytics purposes only. This data helps us improve app performance and functionality. You can review Google’s privacy policy at Google Privacy Policy.

2. In-app Purchases

VolumTransport allows in-app purchases through Google Play Store. All transactions are handled by Google, and we do not have access to your payment information. Google may collect personal data in accordance with its privacy policy and terms of service. You can review Google’s privacy policy at Google Privacy Policy.

3. Ads

The VolumTransport app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect anonymous user data, such as device identifiers or approximate location information, to personalize the ads shown in the app. You can review Google AdMob’s privacy policy at Google AdMob Privacy Policy.

4. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer at volumtransport.graded262@passmail.net.
