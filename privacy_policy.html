VolumTransport Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13/02/2026

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The VolumTransport app does not directly collect, store, or share personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or payment details.

2. In-app Purchases

VolumTransport allows in-app purchases through Google Play Store. All transactions are handled by Google, and we do not have access to your payment information. Google may collect personal data in accordance with its privacy policy and terms of service. You can review Google’s privacy policy at Google Privacy Policy.

3. Ads

The VolumTransport app displays ads provided by Google AdMob and Unity Ads.

Google AdMob may collect anonymous user data, such as device identifiers or approximate location information, to personalize the ads shown in the app. You can review Google AdMob’s privacy policy at Google AdMob Privacy Policy.

Unity Ads may also collect device information, usage data, and other identifiers to provide and optimize advertising. You can review Unity’s privacy policy at Unity Privacy Policy.

4. Use of ARCore (Augmented Reality)

This application runs on Google Play Services for AR (ARCore), which is provided by Google and governed by its Privacy Policy.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer at albherrbri@gmail.com.

6. Analytics and Consent

The VolumTransport app uses Firebase services provided by Google, specifically Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.

  • Firebase Analytics collects anonymized information about how users interact with the app (e.g. screen views, time spent, app usage patterns), which helps us improve the overall experience.
  • Firebase Crashlytics collects technical data when the app encounters an error (e.g. device model, system version, stack trace) to help us detect and fix bugs.

7. Manage Consent Preferences

Users can manage or change their privacy preferences at any time by accessing the "Manage Privacy Preferences" option available in the app’s main menu. This option allows users to review and update their consent regarding analytics and personalized advertising.

Upon first launch, the app will display a consent form that allows users to choose whether they allow data to be collected for analytics and ad personalization purposes. This form complies with the Google EU User Consent Policy.

9. Legal Basis and User Rights (GDPR)

The legal basis for processing data within this application is the user’s consent (Article 6.1.a of the General Data Protection Regulation – GDPR).

The developer does not directly store personally identifiable information. However, certain technical and usage data may be processed by third-party services integrated into the app (such as Google Firebase, Google AdMob, Unity Ads, and ARCore).

Users may request information regarding data processed through these third-party services by contacting the data controller at the email address provided above. Where applicable, users may also exercise their rights directly with the respective service provider in accordance with their privacy policies.
