VolumTransport Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/05/2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the VolumTransport application is used and how data may be processed when you use the app.

The app itself does not directly collect or store personal data such as your name, email address, or payment information. However, it integrates third-party services that may process certain technical or usage data.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect:

  • Name or surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment or billing information

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may process limited data necessary for their functionality:

3.1 Google Play Billing

All in-app purchases are handled securely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment details.

More information: Google Privacy Policy

3.2 Advertising (Google AdMob & Unity Ads)

The app displays ads provided by:

  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Ads

These services may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location (non-precise)
  • Usage data related to ad interactions

This data is used to:

  • Show relevant ads
  • Limit ad frequency
  • Measure ad performance

More information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Unity Privacy Policy

3.3 Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)

The app uses Firebase services:

  • Firebase Analytics: collects anonymized usage data (e.g., screen views, session duration).
  • Firebase Crashlytics: collects technical data when errors occur (e.g., device model, OS version, crash logs).

This information is used exclusively to:

  • Improve app performance
  • Fix errors
  • Enhance user experience

3.4 Google Play Services for AR (ARCore)

This app uses Google Play Services for AR (ARCore), which may process technical data required for augmented reality functionality.

More information: Google Privacy Policy

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), data processing is based on:

  • User consent (Article 6.1.a GDPR) for analytics and personalized ads
  • Legitimate interest (Article 6.1.f GDPR) for basic app functionality and crash reporting

5. User Consent

When you first open the app, you will be presented with a consent form where you can choose:

  • Whether to allow analytics
  • Whether to allow personalized advertising

You can change your preferences at any time from the app menu ("Manage Privacy Preferences").

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal data directly. Third-party services may retain data according to their own policies.

7. International Data Transfers

Some third-party providers (such as Google or Unity) may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

These transfers are protected through appropriate safeguards such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Equivalent legal mechanisms

8. User Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Since data is processed mainly by third parties, you may need to contact them directly. You may also contact us for guidance.
