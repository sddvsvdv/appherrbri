App to calculate how many boxes fit in a vehicle or real space (Android)

VolumTransport (Boxes in the Vehicle) is an Android app designed to calculate how many boxes fit in a van, car, or real space, showing the real 3D placement and allowing you to measure using your phone camera (AR).

Important: this is not a simple volume calculator (CBM). You can see how the boxes are actually placed and whether they really fit, using a 3D simulation based on bin packing techniques, designed for vans, cars, trucks, mixed vehicles, or a universal space mode (custom dimensions).

What you can do with VolumTransport

  • 3D load simulation: rotate and review how space is used.
  • AR measurement: measure boxes or spaces directly with your phone camera.
  • Plan loads for vans, cars, trucks, and mixed vehicles.
  • Universal space mode: storage rooms, rooms, warehouses, or custom spaces.
  • Load balancing across multiple trips: if everything doesn’t fit, help distribute loads to avoid overloads and nearly empty trips.
  • Avoid extra trips by checking in advance if everything fits and how to load it.

Beyond basic load calculation, VolumTransport includes advanced features designed for transport and logistics professionals.

Advantages over Easy Cargo and similar tools

Tools such as Easy Cargo are mainly designed for desktop and web-based environments, with a more technical and industrial focus.

  • VolumTransport is mobile-first: use it directly in the warehouse, on the street, or next to the vehicle.
  • Camera-based AR measurement, no measuring tape or prior data required.
  • More affordable, designed for freelancers and small professionals.
  • Fast and intuitive use, no computer or technical training needed.
  • Ideal for delivery, courier services, moving jobs, and small spaces.

Frequently asked questions

Is it only for vans?

No. It works with vans, cars, trucks, and mixed vehicles.

Is it just a volume (CBM) calculator?

No. It shows the real 3D placement to verify whether the boxes truly fit.

Can I measure with my phone?

Yes. It includes Augmented Reality (AR) measurement.

Can I use it for rooms or storage spaces?

Yes. Use the universal space mode and define custom dimensions.
